My thoughts on Hacknet

2025-11-09

I just finished a game called Hacknet and… WOW

It was surprisingly good. Before playing I thought it’s cool but won’t it be too boring? guess what - it wasn’t.

Gameplay is kinda repetetive but the music as EXTREMELY good.

Story is interesting, the IRC chats often made me laugh.

It was made very smartly, by giving players new executables which makes game progressively more complex - there is not room for boredom.

Even if you are a proggramist, IT guy or tech pro - you should give it a shot.
